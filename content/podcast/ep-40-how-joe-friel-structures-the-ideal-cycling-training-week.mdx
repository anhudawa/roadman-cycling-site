In this episode, we sit down with legendary cycling coach Joe Friel to dive deep into the art and science of building an effective training plan. 

From laying the right foundation to adding intensity and duration, Joe shares his proven methods for riders at every level. We cover how time-crunched athletes can adapt the 80/20 principle, the truth about carb-loading, and how to measure your work rate for maximum gains. Joe also explains durability—why it matters and how to build it—and explores how heat training can boost your VO₂ max. We finish with his all-time favorite session and the biggest training myth he’s ever encountered. Packed with practical tips, this is a masterclass in smart, sustainable cycling performance.
