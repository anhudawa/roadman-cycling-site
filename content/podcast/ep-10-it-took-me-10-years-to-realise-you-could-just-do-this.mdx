Jeremy Powers, the legendary American cyclo-cross cyclist, boasts over 90 UCI victories—the most by any American male rider. His illustrious career includes four USA Cyclocross National Championships (2012, 2014–2016) and the 2015 Pan-American Championship. Beyond racing, Powers has been a driving force in the sport’s growth through the JAM Fund, a non-profit nurturing young talent, and Behind THE Barriers, a video series capturing the wild and passionate world of cyclo-cross.

On the podcast, Jeremy shared insights into the unique culture of European cyclo-cross, the transition from road to CX, and essential tips like mastering tire pressure and skills training. He reflects on life after cycling, discussing identity, his role at GCN, and how now, within his role at WHOOP their wearable tech helps athletes train smarter. A true ambassador of the sport, Powers continues to inspire both on and off the bike.
