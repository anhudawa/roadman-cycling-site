What really happens when you cut your training time in half? 

After 15 years of racing Cat 1 and logging 12–15 hour weeks, life forced me to drop to 6, yep I had to cut my training by 50%. 

Over the past three years, I’ve tracked the exact impact—on my power, weight, racing results, and personal life. This video breaks down the numbers, the science, and the honest trade-offs of trying to stay fast on limited time. From Seiler’s training research to my own power files, this is the unfiltered reality behind “train smarter, not harder.” 

If you’re juggling training with real life, and wondering what you’re giving up—or gaining—this one’s for you.
