Vegan Cyclist (Tyler Pearce ) joins Anthony for another Roadman Cycling Podcast. 

 We delve into the unseen stress that comes with the constant creation of content. As a YouTube content creator with a massive following, Tyler knows firsthand the pressure to consistently produce high-quality videos that engage his audience. We discuss the iconic Impossible Route and where the concept came from. 

This interview is a must-watch for anyone who wants to understand the realities of being a content creator in today's fast-paced digital world.
