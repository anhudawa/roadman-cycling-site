Every cyclist wants to improve their watts per kilo. But is losing weight actually making you slower?

In this episode, I sit down with sports nutritionist and researcher Tim Podlogar to unpack one of the most misunderstood topics in cycling: finding your ideal cycling weight. We dive into power-to-weight ratio, body composition, fueling strategies, and why chasing a lower number on the scale can sometimes backfire.

If you’ve ever cut calories to climb faster — or wondered how lean is too lean — this conversation will change how you think about performance, nutrition, and getting truly race-ready.
