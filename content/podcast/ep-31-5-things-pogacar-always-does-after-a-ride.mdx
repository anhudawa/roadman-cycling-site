Ever wonder what Tadej Pogačar does the moment he crosses the line? In this episode, “The 5 Things Pogacar Always Does After a Ride,” we open the curtain on the first 60 minutes that turn chaos into recovery. 

You’ll see how the WorldTour pros—Pogačar, Vingegaard, Evenepoel, Van der Poel, Van Aert—use active cool-downs, rapid refuelling, functional drinks like tart cherry juice, and instant layering to steer their bodies from race mode into repair. 

We explain the science, the small rituals, and which parts you can copy on a normal budget. From the trainer to the bus, learn how champions protect tomorrow’s legs and how you can build your own simple, consistent post-ride routine to recover faster and ride stronger.
