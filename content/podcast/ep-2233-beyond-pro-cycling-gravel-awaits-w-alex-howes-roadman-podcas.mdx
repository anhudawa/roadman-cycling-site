Alex Howes joins Anthony for another Roadman Cycling Podcast. 

In this inspiring interview with professional cyclist Alex Howes, we explore his decision to retire from pro cycling and embrace the exciting new world of gravel cycling. Join us as we dive deep into his journey from the highly competitive world of road racing to the freedom and adventure of gravel riding. Along the way, we'll discuss the challenges and rewards of pursuing a new discipline, the importance of mental and physical preparation, and the joy of discovering new paths and experiences on two wheels. Whether you're a seasoned cyclist or simply curious about the world of cycling, this conversation is sure to inspire and delight. Get ready to go Beyond Pro Cycling and discover the possibilities of gravel cycling with Alex Howes!
