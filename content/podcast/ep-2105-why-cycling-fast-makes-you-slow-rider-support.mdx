In this weeks Rider Support we break down Dale’s struggle with a stagnating training plan. Next, we settle a friendly debate: Is throwing a banana peel into a rural ditch an eco-friendly move or cycling etiquette faux pas? We also have another question when a listener faces an awkward situation with a friend after a sportive: who’s liable for bike rack damage? We discuss the finer points of cycling etiquette and responsibility.
