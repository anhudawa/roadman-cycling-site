Sofiane Sehili joins Anthony for another Roadman Cycling Podcast.

Sofiane is one of the most accomplished ultra distance bike racers and has toured over 100.000km, visiting more than 40 countries on 5 continents. Sofiane has won some of the most famous ultra distance races in the world.  The Silk Road Mountain Race, Tour Divide, The Atlas Mountain Race & The Inca Divide.  He has become a master of riding for prolonged distances and sleeping very little and these adventures provide us with some very entertaining but extreme stories.
