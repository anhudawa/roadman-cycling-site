Every amateur cyclist falls into the same trap: chasing intensity instead of consistency. In this episode, I sit down with Dr. Christian Schrot, performance coach at Team Jayco AlUla, to explore one of endurance sport’s most surprising truths — riding easy is what actually makes you faster. 

We unpack the science behind the 80/20 polarized training model, why zone discipline matters, and how even elite pros struggle with holding back. 

From common misconceptions to the training habits that really work, this conversation is a must for any cyclist who’s ever asked, “Am I training hard enough?” Spoiler: if you’re doing it right, most of your rides should feel almost too easy.
