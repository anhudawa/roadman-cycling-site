Todays guest is Jérémie Debeuf who is the Head Of Product at Van Rysel bikes. The cycling community has been totally captivated by the new kid in town - Decathlon World Tour cycling and the success they have had in the peloton on these bikes. Van Rysel have totally overhauled their production of what was previously viewed as a budget bike and produced an amazing product that sold out within hours of being released to the public. This podcast lifts the lid on how Van Rysel have been so successful and Anthony goes deep into the nitty gritty of the bike and asks "how fast is it?".
