Ever wondered what’s really inside those mysterious green shots you see World Tour drinking before big races? In this video, I dive into the buzz behind these tiny bottles – dubbed by many as the pros’ new secret drink. Riders claim it helps them push harder, delay lactate buildup, and reduce the burn. But is it hype or science? I sit down with leading sports physiologist Dr. Filip Larsen to unpack what’s actually in these shots, how they’re supposed to work, and even design a test to see if they really improve my own performance.

By partnering with British Cycling, Lloyds is helping more people experience the joy

of riding and showing how those small personal pedal strokes can move us all forward.

So if you haven’t already, maybe now is the time to get on a bike yourself. Lloyds, there

at every stage.
