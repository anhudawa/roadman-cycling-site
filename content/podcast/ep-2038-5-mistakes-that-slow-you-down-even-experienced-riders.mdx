Think you’ve got your cycling dialled in? Think again. In this episode, Anthony and Sarah break down five of the most common cycling mistakes that even experienced riders still make — and how to fix them. 

From under-fueling and grinding the wrong gear to pacing misfires, poor recovery, and skipping strength work, we dive into the habits that are quietly holding your performance back. 

Expect science-backed advice, practical fixes, and a few personal confessions along the way. Whether you’re new to the sport or chasing marginal gains, this episode is your blueprint for smarter, stronger riding. Because sometimes, knowing what not to do is the fastest way to level up.
