I decided to fully commit to training like a professional cyclist for 60 days — no shortcuts, no excuses. Structured workouts, long hours on the bike, strict recovery, and a whole new level of discipline. I wanted to see what would actually change: my fitness, my mindset, my body, and my relationship with training. Some things were expected… others definitely weren’t. In this video, I break down what the experience was really like, what surprised me the most, and whether training like a pro is actually worth it for everyday riders. This isn’t a transformation story — it’s an honest experiment.
