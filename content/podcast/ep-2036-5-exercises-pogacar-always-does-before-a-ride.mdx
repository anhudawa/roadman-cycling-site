What do Tadej Pogačar and top physiotherapists agree on? Pre-ride activation and post-ride exercises aren’t optional—they’re essential. In this episode, we dive into the evidence-backed routines that can unlock better performance, reduce injury risk, and keep you riding strong as you age. From mini-band glute work to post-ride stretches that release your hips and back, this is your practical guide to staying fast, mobile, and pain-free on the bike.
