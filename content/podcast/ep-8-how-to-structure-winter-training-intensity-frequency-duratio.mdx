We’ve just started to build something really cool. Want to be one of the first to join? 👉 [https://www.skool.com/roadmancycling/about](https://www.skool.com/roadmancycling/about)

Winter training can set you up for your best summer—or leave you cooked before racing even starts. In this episode, I’m joined by Daryl Fitzgerald from Science to Sport to break pro training down to the three levers that actually drive results: intensity, frequency, and duration. We’ll look at how top riders build their base, where intensity really fits (and where it doesn’t), why easy riding—yes, even Zone 1—can be a serious performance tool, and how to stack hard sessions without digging a hole you can’t recover from. If you want a clear, practical framework to train smarter this winter, this one’s for you.
