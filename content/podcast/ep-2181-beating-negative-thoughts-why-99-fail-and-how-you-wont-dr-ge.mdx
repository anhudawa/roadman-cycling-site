Dr Michael Gervais joins Anthony for another Roadmat Podcast to talk about how 99% of people fail to quieten their negative voice and how you can be different.

He's the man behind the 'Finding Mastery' podcast and has levelled up the greatest athletes in the world from his work in the NFL with the Seattle Sea Hawks, to UFC Fighters and even US Tier 1 Military Operators.  This guy knows what it takes to perform at the pinnacle of human ability.

In today's episode, we discuss the internal narratives that hold us back, the power of self-talk, and practical strategies to revamp your internal dialogue for a healthier, happier, and more fulfilling life.
