Daryl Impey joins Anthony for another Roadman Cycling Podcast. 

Daryl Impey," the South African pro cyclist discusses the challenges and rewards of his sport, including the risks involved and how his approach to risk-taking has changed over time. Impey, an 11-time South African national champion and two-time winner of the Tour Down Under, shares his thoughts on his friendship with Chris Froome, the possibility of Pogacar surpassing Froome's success, and the lack of respect among riders in the peloton. Get a rare and candid look into the world of pro cycling from one of its most accomplished riders.
