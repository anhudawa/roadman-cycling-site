Sarah and Anthony unpack Badlands—the notorious self-supported gravel epic across Andalucía. 

They dig into what makes it addictive (silence, heat, endless ramblas) and how to ride smart—whether you want the pointy end or just a finish. Expect real-world gear talk (1x vs 2x, smart gearing choices), navigation that won’t fail at 3 a.m., tyre and wheel picks for sand and sharp rock, and a no-fluff packing list that actually fits. 

The route breakdown covers water gaps, night-riding strategy, coastal wind, and those rude hike-a-bike stings near the mountains. It’s romance meets reality—perfect prep for your first ultra.
