What does a day in the life of a pro cyclist really look like?

In this episode, I sit down with one of the fastest-rising talents in professional cycling, EF Education–EasyPost’s Darren Rafferty. On the surface, you see a calm, composed rider lining up at races like the Giro d’Italia. But behind the scenes is a world of pressure, expectation, and sacrifice that few ever talk about.

From growing up in Ireland to navigating brutal WorldTour setbacks and breakthrough wins, Darren opens up about the realities of life inside one of the top teams in the world. This is pro cycling, unfiltered.
