Ed Veal, Professional Cyclist, joined me on the podcast a few months ago and it proved to be one of our most popular podcasts ever so we had to get him back.  

In this episode Ed admits that he can be intense and passionate about getting the best out of himself and the people around him which some people can handle and others can't.  

It's been a crazy time for Ed since as he recently had a very nasty crash and we talk about that, his recovery, how he has used cycling as a form of therapy his entire life and so much more. 

Not only is Ed super human at recovering from high speed crashes, he is an insightful, thoughtful and interesting person.  Ed has accomplished so much in cycling; Collecting International Caps, Winning Races and bagging some pretty insane records but what is most impressive is his outlook on life.
