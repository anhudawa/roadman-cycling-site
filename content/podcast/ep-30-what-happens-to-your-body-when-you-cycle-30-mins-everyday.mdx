## Key Takeaways

- A 70kg person cycling 30 mins each way (5 hours/week) at moderate intensity burns 2,000-3,000 calories—enough to lose roughly 1 pound per week without changing diet
- Shift early on climbs before you lose speed, ease off pedal pressure momentarily when shifting, and avoid cross-chaining to eliminate clunking noises and maintain momentum
- In group rides, maintain consistent pace when at the front, ride at Zone 3 effort, and communicate clearly—surging happens because riders aren't concentrating or reading the group
- Tubeless tyres catch small punctures with sealant automatically, but require a special pump, new wheels/tyres, and messy roadside tube changes if you get a big puncture
- AI coaching works best as a research assistant (correlating Whoop and TrainingPeaks data) for mid-level cyclists; human coaches remain essential for top-end athletes who need pattern recognition during bad days
