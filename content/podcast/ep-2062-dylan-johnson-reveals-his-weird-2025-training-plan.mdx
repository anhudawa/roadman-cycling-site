Anthony welcomes Dylan Johnson back to the podcast for a deep dive into the nuts and bolts of endurance training. 

Dylan breaks down how he structures his own training plan, from the big-picture macro cycles all the way down to the finer details of meso cycles. 

They discuss how to balance intensity and volume, why tapering is often misunderstood, and how to time it right for race day. Dylan also shares his approach to strength training—how much, how often, and where it fits into the schedule of an endurance athlete. Whether you’re looking to get faster, avoid burnout, or finally nail your race prep, Dylan’s insight into training smarter (not just harder) is packed with valuable takeaways.
