In this episode, Anthony breaks down the simple framework that can make your winter training block far more effective: getting the dose, frequency, and duration right for where you are right now. Instead of chasing random workouts, you’ll learn how to build consistency, stack the right sessions in the right order, and progress week to week without burning out. Anthony also shares practical ways to shape your training so it supports stronger fitness, better resilience, and a smoother transition into higher-intensity work later in the year. A clear, no-fuss approach to setting yourself up for a strong summer.
