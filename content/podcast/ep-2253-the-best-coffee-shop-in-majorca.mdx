We're diving into one of cycling's most legendary coffee stops in Majorca. This isn't just any café—it's where pro cyclists regularly gather, and the walls tell the story of cycling greatness. ## Key Takeaways

- Cycling Alanis in Majorca is a must-visit spot for cyclists in the region, frequented by professional riders and decorated with memorabilia from world champions
- The coffee shop's owner is David Montaigne, an ex-world champion, lending serious credibility to the establishment
- The walls feature signed photos and memorabilia from elite cyclists including Derek Martens, Tony Martin, Mark Cavendish, and other champions—a visual history of cycling excellence
- Visiting Cycling Alanis provides both quality coffee and the chance to soak in cycling culture and history in one spot
- The vlogger recommends making it part of your recovery routine when cycling in Majorca, pairing it with local climbs to explore
