Shannon Malseed joins Anthony for another Roadman Podcast. Today we explore how to break free of the 99% trap, the power transformation, and breaking free from life's self-imposed shackles. This is for anyone who has ever felt confined - be it in a career, a relationship, or a limiting mindset. Prepare to be inspired.

🚴‍♀️ Joining us today is Shannon Malseed, a name synonymous with resilience and transformation. Formerly a professional cyclist with one of the world's most prestigious teams, Shannon reached a turning point in her life. A point where the path laid out before her no longer aligned with her inner truth.

🌟 In this revealing episode, Shannon shares her incredible journey from being a top athlete to becoming a beacon of inspiration and guidance. She delves into how she confronted and dismantled the emotional barriers and self-imposed limits that once defined her life.

💡 Shannon doesn't just share her story; she offers actionable insights and practical techniques for anyone looking to undergo a profound personal transformation. From understanding the concept of being 'Limitless' to real strategies for aligning your life with your true self, this conversation is a treasure trove of wisdom.
