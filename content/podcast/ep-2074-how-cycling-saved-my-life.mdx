In this heartfelt conversation, I sit down with my childhood friend, Ciaran O’Conluain, to reflect on how cycling has shaped our lives. From growing up together and going to school to embarking on our cycling journeys side by side, we dive deep into the impact the sport has had on both of us. We share the mistakes we made along the way, the lessons we learned, and the regrets we hold, all while discussing what we would do differently if we could go back. This conversation is a nostalgic and thoughtful exploration of the power of cycling to transform lives, with plenty of real, raw reflections along the way.
