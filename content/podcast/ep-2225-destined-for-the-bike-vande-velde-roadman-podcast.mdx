Christian Vande Velde  joins Anthony for another Roadman Cycling Podcast. 

Christian  raced at the highest level throughout his 15-year career in the pro peloton, he has competed at 2008 Summer Olympics and a whopping  18 Grand Tours. With two top-10 performances and multiple team victories including a 4th place in the Tour De France in 2008 Christian career was inspirational. Post-retirement, Christian is a NBC sports commentator & entrepreneur and in this chat with Anthony about being the first to use bike computers, skin suits and how the sport has evolved.
