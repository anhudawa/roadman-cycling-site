In this episode, I sit down with John Archibald, one of the UK's fastest time trial athletes, to dive deep into the strategies and techniques behind his success in the discipline. John is known for his remarkable time trial performances and is a dominant figure in the UK time trial scene, holding some of the fastest times across multiple distances.

During our chat, we cover everything from his training structure to pacing strategies, and dive into the technical details that go into optimizing his performance. John opens up about his approach to balancing volume and intensity in training, and shares the interval sessions that help him improve time trial performance. We also talk about the importance of recovery, strength training, and how he incorporates indoor versus outdoor training to keep his edge.
