Benji Naesen  joins Anthony for another Roadman Cycling Podcast. 

Benji Naesen has quickly become an authority on cycling and his commentary, knowledge and ability to really study and understand numbers and nuances of riders and teams but he has p*ssed off a few within cycling along the way! The old school cycling fraternity have not all welcomed him with open arms. 

His background in computer programming seems an unlikely asset to sports commentating but that attention to detail really sets him apart in his understanding of the pro peloton.  Anthony and Benji chat his struggle with weight and how he is now, not only a spectator but, a cyclist too.
