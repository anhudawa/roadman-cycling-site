Join us in this exclusive interview with Mark Beaumont, the endurance cyclist who pushed the limits of human potential by cycling around the world in just 80 days, setting a new world record. In this in-depth conversation, Mark shares the highs and lows of his incredible journey, the meticulous planning involved, and the mental and physical challenges he faced along the way.

Discover how Mark managed the daunting task of cycling an average of 240 miles a day and the strategies he used to maintain his pace against all odds. From battling extreme weather to navigating logistical hurdles and maintaining his mental health, Mark's story is one of extraordinary determination and resilience.

🚴 Learn about the equipment and technology that supported his journey.

🌍 Get insights into the cultures and landscapes he encountered.

🔧 Hear firsthand how he overcame unexpected challenges.
