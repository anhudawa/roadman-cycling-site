Sick of politics and just want to talk cycling? We built something free to help [https://www.skool.com/roadman](https://www.skool.com/roadman)  to sign up to our FREE cycling community! 

In today’s episode, Anthony sits down with Michael “Bling” Matthews — a WorldTour mainstay since 2011 — to unpack what pros learn the hard way, and what amateurs rarely get told. Matthews reflects on the difference between knowing what to do and building the repeatable habits that actually win races: training structure, recovery discipline, and nutrition that holds up under pressure. Along the way, we touch on some of his biggest results — including winning the Tour de France points (green) jersey in 2017, multiple Tour stage wins, a Giro d’Italia stage win (returning to victory there in 2023), and major one-day wins like GP Québec and GP Montréal.
