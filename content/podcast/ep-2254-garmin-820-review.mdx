In this video I review the Garmin 820. I've been a Garmin user for many years but recently have tested the Garmin against Wahoo and other head units. The 820, for me, is a nice compromise between the functionality of the Garmin 1000 and the style/form of the 500 Series. 

The 820 is a really nice unit and is packed with some features that you wouldn't expect.
