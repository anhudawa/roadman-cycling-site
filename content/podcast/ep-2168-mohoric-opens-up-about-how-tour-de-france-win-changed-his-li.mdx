Mohoric has left an indelible mark on the cycling world, showcasing his prowess with remarkable victories since a young age, clinching junior and under-23 world championships. He has consistently lived up to his early potential, securing triumphs in prestigious races such as Milan San Remo and stages across every grand tour. Notably, he currently holds the esteemed title of the world gravel champion.

In this episode, we delve into Matej's world, embarking on a journey through his experiences, navigating the peaks and valleys of his career, understanding his racing philosophy, and gaining insights from his illustrious cycling journey.
