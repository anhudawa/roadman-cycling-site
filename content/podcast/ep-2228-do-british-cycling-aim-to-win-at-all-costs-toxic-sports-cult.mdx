Do British Cycling win at all costs?  - toxic sports culture 

We speak with British professional cyclist Fred Wright to explore the toxic sports culture of British Cycling and the "win at all costs" mentality that permeates the sport.

Through our conversation with Fred and insights from experts, we examine the impact of this toxic culture on the mental and physical health of athletes and the wider cycling community. We also discuss the role of leadership in fostering a healthy sports culture that prioritizes athlete well-being and safety over winning at all costs.
