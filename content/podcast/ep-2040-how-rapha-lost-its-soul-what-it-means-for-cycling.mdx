Together they pulled the brand away from the thing that made people pay £100 for a pair of shorts in the first place.

Simon Mottram stepped down as CEO at the end of 2021. A fashion executive came in after him. By late 2023, Fran Miller took over, sister of former WorldTour rider David Millar, previously managed Team Sky and Ineos. The North American office closed. Rapha Travel shut down. Staff were cut. The core business may be close to breaking even before exceptional charges, the company claimed £3 million in restructuring costs in the most recent accounts, but those charges are real money going out the door. The question now is whether Miller can reconnect Rapha with the riders who made it worth £200 million in the first place.
