Netflix spent €8 million to bring the Tour de France to the world. But for many inside the sport, the result wasn’t just disappointing — it was a betrayal. In this episode, we break down exactly where Unchained went wrong: fake drama, key moments cut, and riders like Wout van Aert and Tom Pidcock misrepresented. 

With only 8 of 22 teams agreeing to return for Season 2, the message from the peloton was clear — this isn’t the sport they live and breathe. From the meaning of the yellow jersey to the unwritten rules Netflix ignored, this is a deep dive into how the world’s most beautiful sport was misunderstood… and why it matters.
