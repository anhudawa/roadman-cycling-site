What’s the real secret to pro-level gains? In this episode, we sit down with AG2R Decathlon’s head coach, Stephen Barrett, to uncover what pro cyclists are doing that you aren’t. From training structure to power numbers, Stephen breaks it all down — including never-before-seen data from Felix Gall’s Tour de France performance. How close was he to riders like Vingegaard and Pogacar? The answer might shock you. 

We also dive into the unique challenges of getting Irish sprinter Sam Bennett back to the top. Whether you're chasing your own cycling gains or just curious what separates amateurs from the pros, this deep dive has the insights you've been missing. If you want to train smarter, this episode is essential.
