For years we have been obsessed with heart rate monitors and power meter data but what if there is a new way to measure fitness and set training - Your breathing.  

Today Anthony sits down to chat with Arnar Larusson founder of Tymewear,  a technology that is already being used by Team Visma Lease A Bike.  This tech lets the user establish new and more accurate training zones!  This innovation has the potential to be the biggest breakthrough since heart rate first hit the scene.  So if you want to know how your next secret weapon is as simple as breathing better listen up!
