In today's podcast Anthony chats to Sean Landers who is one of Irelands leading cyclists. Tragically Seans' girlfriend Gabby who was also a top Irish cyclist was killed by a motorist on a training ride. 

Sean opens up about the loss, his dark moments, his mission to end cyclist road deaths and he speaks about Gabby and what a phenomenal person the world has lost way too soon. 

Sean would like anyone going through similar tough times, that is in need of someone to talk to to reach out to him at anytime on Instagram.
