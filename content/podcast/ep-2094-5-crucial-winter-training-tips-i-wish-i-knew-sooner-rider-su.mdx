Welcome to this week’s episode of Rider Support where we dive into the crucial steps every cyclist should take before embarking on their winter training journey. Whether you're gearing up to maintain your form or planning to build strength during the colder months, this episode is packed with invaluable advice to set you up for success.

In this episode, you'll discover:

Five Crucial Pre-Winter Training Tips: Learn the key preparations and adjustments you need to make before you start your winter regimen. From setting up your indoor training environment to specific nutritional adjustments, these tips are designed to help you train effectively and stay motivated even when it's cold outside.

Training Progression and CTL Ramp Rate: Understand how fast you should progress your training over the winter. We discuss CTL (Chronic Training Load) ramp rates in detail, helping you find the right balance between increasing your fitness and avoiding burnout.

Power Meters – Finding Accuracy: Dive into the world of power meters with us as we analyze which models offer the most accuracy and reliability. Make an informed decision about which device might be the best investment for your training needs.
