Dr Andrew Pruitt joins Anthony for another Roadman Cycling Podcast.

Dr Pruitt is the godfather of bike fitting. He created many of the system (RETUL) which we now rely on to optimise bike position. 

In this conversation we talk about how to alleviate common cycling pains like neck, back & knee. We also chat about the evolution of bike fitting industry and what tools you can use to do a "next gen" bike fit from your own home.
