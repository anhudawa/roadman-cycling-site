Matt Hayman joins Anthony for another Roadman Cycling Podcast. 

The Australian former professional cyclist known for his incredible win at the 2016 Paris-Roubaix. We dive deep into the details of that historic race, discussing the tactics, the mindset, and the physical challenges he faced. But we also explore the earlier chapters of his career, including his time as a domestique for top teams like Rabobank and Sky, and his multiple national championships in Australia. Beyond his racing achievements, we also learn about his personal life, his family, and his passion for coaching and mentoring young riders. And finally, we talk about his retirement from professional cycling and his plans for the future, which include continuing to work with young talents and transition into DS roles. Whether you're a cycling fan or not, this conversation offers fascinating insights into the world of elite sports and the mindset of a true champion.
