This video dives deep into one of cycling’s most dramatic breakups: Trek and Greg LeMond. Once a powerful partnership that helped define American cycling, it all unraveled when LeMond spoke out about doping in the sport—specifically Lance Armstrong and TREK silenced him. What followed was a battle of silence, power, and truth. From court filings to press statements, we uncover why Trek dropped LeMond, what it cost him, and how the industry reacted. Was it about business—or protecting a myth? This isn’t just about bikes—it’s about who gets to control the story.
