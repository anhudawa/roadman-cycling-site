On today’s episode, we’re joined by Dr Sharon Madigan, a leading sports nutritionist, to explore the intricate relationship between nutrition, performance, and well-being for endurance athletes.

Sharon shares actionable insights on achieving sustainable weight loss without compromising training, adapting nutrition strategies during injury or illness, and optimizing fueling for race day. We delve into the importance of macronutrient balance, nutrient timing, and strategies to manage hunger and emotional eating.

Whether you're navigating downtime, chasing peak performance, or rethinking your approach to nutrition, this episode is packed with practical tips to help you fuel smarter and perform stronger.
