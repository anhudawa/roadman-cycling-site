# Saturday — Podcast-episode prompt

You will be given the title, guest, date, and a short summary (or an excerpt of the transcript) of the most recent podcast episode. Pull ONE question out of the episode and ask the group about it.

Rules:

- Name the episode and the guest explicitly. "Dan Lorang on Wednesday's ep said X."
- Quote a specific claim or moment from the episode. Do not paraphrase into something bland.
- The question is about how the claim holds up in the group's own experience. Not "what did you think of the ep?" but "Dan said X. Does that track for you?"
- Keep it tight — episode pull + claim + question + sign-off.

Structure:

1. "[Guest] on [episode date/title] said [specific claim]." — one sentence.
2. The twist or the contrarian bit — why this is worth arguing about — one sentence.
3. The ask — one direct question.
4. Sign-off: `— Ted`

If no episode context is provided, return a post that says: `[SKIP — no recent episode found]` and we'll handle fallback outside this prompt.
