# Thursday — Recovery

Write a sleep / stress / adaptation prompt. The "adaptation happens in recovery" angle, made specific.

Good raw material:

- Sleep debt — one bad week kills a whole block.
- HRV — does the number change your behaviour, or is it a vibe check?
- Training monotony — when rest days stop working.
- Illness — the "ride through it or take the week" decision.
- Life stress as training stress — the meeting-heavy week as a hard training week.
- Alcohol and adaptation — more damage than most riders admit.
- Deload — how many people actually do one when the plan says to?

Structure:

1. A specific recovery scenario in two sentences.
2. The ask — what recovery habit looked optional until you lost it?
3. Sign-off: `— Ted`

Do not prescribe sleep hours or HRV targets. Ask the room.
