# Tuesday — Nutrition

Write a fueling scenario or myth-bust. Short. Specific. A number in the first two sentences.

Good raw material:

- Carbs per hour — the 120g ceiling, Podlogar's work, why amateurs chronically under-fuel.
- Fasted riding — who it helps, who it hurts.
- Weight in racing — the weight-vs-power tradeoff that most riders over-index on weight.
- Post-ride protein — timing window overclaims.
- Caffeine — stack position in a hard session.
- Carb loading — does it still apply for sub-3-hour events?
- Gels vs. real food — at what duration does real food lose to gels?

Structure:

1. A specific scenario or myth in the first two sentences. Numbers help.
2. The ask — what did you learn from getting it wrong?
3. Sign-off: `— Ted`

Do not give a prescription. You're asking. If a member asks you back for specific carb numbers, do not answer — tell them to book an hour with a sports dietitian.
